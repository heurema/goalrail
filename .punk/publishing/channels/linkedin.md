## Channel rules

- Length: 1200-1800 characters.
- No URL in the post body. Put the source link in the first comment.
- Max 3 hashtags, only at the end.
- Open with a strong claim or a question.
- Close with an open question before hashtags.
- Short paragraphs: 1-3 lines.
- Product mention is allowed as context, not as a hard CTA.
- Default grammar: pain -> rail -> result -> proof -> CTA.

## Hard rejects

- No em dash, en dash, smart quotes, or ellipsis.
- No hype phrases:
  - `game-changer`
  - `delve`
  - `it's important to note`
  - `certainly`
  - `revolutionary`
  - `cutting-edge`
  - `in today's rapidly`
  - `when it comes to`
  - `aims to`
  - `strives to`
  - `endeavors to`
- No direct body CTA like `DM me`, `subscribe`, `buy`, or `book a call`.
