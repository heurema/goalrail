# Image prompt: LinkedIn post image, episode 1

Create a premium editorial illustration for a LinkedIn post series about Goalrail / Goal on Rails. This is the first image in a series, so make it feel like a strong visual anchor that future illustrations can continue from. The image should tell the story clearly even without reading the post.

Use the real Goalrail demo admin screenshot as the primary UI style and branding reference. Match its dark graphite-to-brown interface theme, warm amber accents, subtle panel borders, minimal typography feel, and overall Goalrail visual language. On the large monitor in the illustration, show a simplified but clearly Goalrail-inspired interface, with contract/workflow panels, progress stages, dark UI cards, and amber highlights.

Use the conceptual composition as storytelling reference: a thoughtful person at a desk, seen from behind or three-quarter back, looking at a large monitor; a rail-like workflow with checkpoints flowing toward the screen; an editorial, business-oriented illustration style; clean composition and symbolic visual storytelling.

Vertical 4:5 composition optimized for a LinkedIn post image. Show a single focused professional at a desk, thoughtful and observant, looking at a large screen. The screen shows a Goalrail-style workspace or contract flow UI. A rail / track / guided path visually moves from the left side toward the monitor, symbolizing Goal on Rails. The path should include symbolic checkpoint nodes such as goal / contract, checklist or clarification, testing or verification, proof / approval. The rails should visually suggest guidance and bounded execution.

Style: modern editorial illustration, sophisticated, polished, business-oriented, not comic, not cyberpunk, not childish. Palette: dark graphite, charcoal, deep brown, muted beige, warm amber/gold highlights, soft off-white accents.
