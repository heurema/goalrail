# Knowledge

Repo-tracked advisory knowledge for Goalrail.

Use this area for material that informs the product but does not become canon until promoted.

## Layout

- `knowledge/research/` — curated research notes and prior-art summaries
- `knowledge/ideas/` — speculative backlog and loose idea capture
- `knowledge/_templates/` — starter templates
- `knowledge/_schema/` — future metadata/schema helpers

Promotion rule:
- research and ideas are advisory until promoted into `docs/product/`, `docs/ops/`, or ADRs
